My Opinion on Sustainability in Engineering

I personally believe that complete sustainability is not achievable in our civilization. What I mean by that is that it will be impossible to create or come up with a solution which leaves no negative foot print behind.

Take for example the current push to the Net-zero emissions. While in theory the push seeks to oppose the negative effects of carbon emissions it will be an extremely cumbersome task to achieve efficiently. Moreover, inorder for facilitate for these rehabilitation projects, fossil fuels will likely be used for the remaning of the near future - which diminishes the topsoil conditions and further complicates the task for future generations. 

Therefore, I believe that the role of an Engineer is to aim to minimize the negative bi-products of his/her objective while seeking to maximizing the lifetime of their project - rather than creating a solution which produces no negative bi-products.
