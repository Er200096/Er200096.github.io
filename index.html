Let's talk sustainability 🌿

1. Use of recyclable / biodegradable  materials

The bridge is entirely built from recyclable materials and or is biodegradable.  These include the use of wooden popsicle sticks and PLA material. 

2. Easy to repair

Given the overall simplicity of the design, its easy to repair and configure. This means that less material is required for long-term maintenance. 

3. Open source and replicable 

The K-truss bridge is a well known and open source design. Moreover, the 3D printed parts are also can be made openly available and are highly replicable. Because of this, the bridge can be maintained for long periods of time by a range of engineers. 

What we learnt from this project 🤝

This project provided us with a deeper insight of civil engineering and analyzing forces in structures. This project has also helped us to further develop our collaborative and communication skills.
